# Pretentious Words → Simple Replacements (Заумное → Просто)

Based on the principles of «Пиши, сокращай».

---

## Core Principle

If a simple Russian word exists, use it. Foreign and complex words create distance between the author and reader. They don't make the text smarter — they make it harder to read.

**Rule:** «Не уверены в слове — не пишите.»

**Detection rule:** If you catch yourself using a word that you wouldn't say in a casual conversation with a friend, it's probably pretentious. Replace it.

AI-generated text is especially prone to using complex borrowed words because it mimics formal, "impressive" writing patterns. In Russian, this creates a particularly cold, unnatural tone.

## Important Exceptions

Not all borrowed words are bad. Keep them when:

1. **No exact Russian equivalent exists** — «компьютер», «интернет», «менеджер» (in some contexts)
2. **The audience expects technical terminology** — «API», «сервер», «бэкенд» in dev docs
3. **The simple word changes the meaning** — «концепция» and «идея» are not always interchangeable in technical contexts
4. **The word is established in the domain** — «оптимизация» in SEO context, «интеграция» in software

**Rule of thumb:** If the text is for a general audience, always prefer the simple word. If the text is for specialists in a narrow domain, keep established terms but simplify everything around them.

## AI-Specific Amplification

AI-generated Russian text uses pretentious words at 2–3x the rate of human writing. Watch for clusters:

| AI-heavy | Human |
|----------|-------|
| Имплементация инновационных решений для оптимизации бизнес-процессов | Внедрили новые решения, чтобы работать быстрее |
| Трансформация парадигмы коммуникации | Мы изменили подход к общению |
| Верификация и валидация детализированной концепции | Проверили, что идея работает |

If you see 3+ pretentious words in a single sentence, the sentence almost certainly needs a full rewrite, not just word-for-word replacement.

## Quick Self-Test

Read the sentence aloud. Would you say this to a colleague over coffee? If not, simplify.

«Лучше просто об умном, чем сложно о ерунде»
